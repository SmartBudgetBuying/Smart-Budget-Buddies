Best Budget Smartphones Under ₹15,000 in 2024

Last Updated: January 15, 2024

Introduction

Finding the best smartphone on a budget can be challenging with so many options available in the market. In this comprehensive review, we've tested and compared the top budget-friendly smartphones under ₹15,000 to help you make an informed decision. These phones offer excellent value for money without compromising on essential features like performance, camera quality, and battery life.

What to Look for in a Budget Smartphone

Before diving into our recommendations, let's understand the key factors to consider when choosing a budget smartphone:

  • Processor: Look for a processor that handles daily tasks smoothly
  • RAM: At least 4GB RAM for smooth multitasking
  • Storage: 64GB or more for apps and media
  • Camera: Decent rear and front cameras for photos and videos
  • Battery: 5000mAh or more for all-day usage
  • Display: IPS LCD or AMOLED with good brightness

Top Budget Smartphones Under ₹15,000

1. Redmi Note 12

The Redmi Note 12 is an excellent choice for budget-conscious buyers looking for a reliable smartphone with good performance and camera capabilities.

  • Processor: MediaTek Helio G88
  • RAM: 4GB / 6GB options
  • Storage: 64GB / 128GB
  • Display: 6.67-inch IPS LCD, 90Hz refresh rate
  • Camera: 50MP rear, 13MP front
  • Battery: 5000mAh with 33W charging

Pros: Great display, good battery life, reliable performance, good camera quality

Cons: Average processor, no 5G support

2. Samsung Galaxy A13

Samsung's Galaxy A13 offers a clean Android experience with reliable performance and a good display for everyday use.

  • Processor: Exynos 850
  • RAM: 4GB / 6GB options
  • Storage: 64GB / 128GB
  • Display: 6.6-inch IPS LCD, 90Hz refresh rate
  • Camera: 50MP rear, 8MP front
  • Battery: 5000mAh with 15W charging

Pros: Samsung brand reliability, clean UI, good build quality, long software support

Cons: Slower charging, average processor

3. Poco M4

The Poco M4 delivers excellent value with a high refresh rate display and solid performance for gaming and multitasking.

  • Processor: MediaTek Helio G99
  • RAM: 4GB / 6GB options
  • Storage: 64GB / 128GB
  • Display: 6.43-inch AMOLED, 90Hz refresh rate
  • Camera: 50MP rear, 20MP front
  • Battery: 5000mAh with 33W charging

Pros: AMOLED display, good performance, excellent value, fast charging

Cons: Limited software updates, average low-light camera performance

Our Recommendation

For the best overall value, we recommend the Redmi Note 12. It offers an excellent balance of performance, display quality, camera capabilities, and battery life. The 90Hz display is smooth and responsive, the camera takes good photos in various lighting conditions, and the battery easily lasts a full day with moderate usage.

If you prefer a more premium feel and AMOLED display, the Poco M4 is an excellent choice despite being at the higher end of the budget range.

Final Thoughts

Budget smartphones have come a long way, and today you can get a reliable device with good performance and features under ₹15,000. The phones we've reviewed offer excellent value for money and are suitable for everyday tasks, social media, photography, and light gaming.

Consider your priorities—whether it's display quality, camera performance, or processing power—and choose the phone that best fits your needs and budget.
